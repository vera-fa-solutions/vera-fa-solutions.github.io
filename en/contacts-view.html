                                                Contacts view

                                                The Contacts view allows you to search for contacts saved in the system. You can perform a completely new contact search, or use the contact search creteria saved earlier. The Contacts view also allows you to export the data as XLS or PDF or generate a report from the search view.Exporting data as XLSX or PDFGenerate reports for transactions and trade orders

                                                Searching for contacts

                                                The Contacts view allows you to search for contacts. You can:

                                                • Quick search by typing in your search criteria into the available field.Quick search and filtering

                                                • Click "Show all" and search through filtering.Quick search and filtering

                                                • "Show extended search" and define your search criteria in the available fields.

                                                • Access one of the saved search tabs to do a pre-defined search.Saving search views

                                                The contacts found are listed in the table.

                                                You can also save the search for later use as a new tab in the Contacts view. Saving the search saves the search criteria, and the search with the search criteria is conducted again every time the saved search is opened.Saving search views

                                                Viewing the contacts in the table

                                                The columns show the contact information you store in the system, as well as the created and last modified dates.

                                                To choose which columns you want to use, click in the upper-right corner of the screen. To sort the contacts, click the column headers: the arrow next to the column header indicates that the contacts are ordered based on the values in that column.

                                                Double-clicking a contact from the search result opens the contact information in the Contact window for viewing and editing.

                                                Processes

                                                In addition, you can perform different processes against the contacts searched on the Contacts view. These processes are shown on the bottom of the Contacts view as buttons. These processes are always started against a contact search: the processes affect the contacts searched on the view.

                                                Processes configured against "contacts" are visible at the bottom of the Contacts view.
