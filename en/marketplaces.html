                          Marketplaces

                          The standard solution has a list of marketplaces according to the ISO standard. You can see the complete list in this Excel:

                          ISO10383_MarketPlaces.xls

                          You can add a Market place from Preference → Securities → Market place.

                          All marketplaces have a similar structure:

                          You can specify a marketplace in the security, trade order or transaction information. To do this, start typing the marketplace name or MIC code in the Market place field.
