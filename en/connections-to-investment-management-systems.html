                      Connections to Investment Management Systems

                      FA allows you to connect to other investment management systems used for trading and trade order management, and receive information about executed trades.

                      Functionality

                      Exchange information needed for trading

                      • Send cash balance information – automatically send cash positions per account and cash flows per account to another investment management platform where you create and execute trade orders.

                      • Receive information about executed trades – receive transactions and position reconciliation data from the investment management system.

                      Update investment information in FA

                      • Automatically update portfolio positions – have portfolio positions automatically updated in FA based on the data received from the investment management system.
